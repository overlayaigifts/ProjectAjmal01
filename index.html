Since 1951 · Dubai, UAE

Seventy years of
oud, heritage & soul

From a single shop in Ajman to 300+ fragrances loved across 50 countries — this is the story of a family's obsession with perfection.

1951
The first shop in Ajman
Haji Ajmal Ali opens a modest attar shop in the UAE — hand-blending traditional Arabian fragrances for the local community. Every bottle is personal, every customer a neighbor.
1 Shop, one family, one obsession
1970s
The oud expeditions begin
Ajmal's founders travel personally to Assam, India — forging direct relationships with oud farmers and establishing ethical sourcing practices that still define the brand today.
40+ Years of direct farmer partnerships
1990s
From regional to international
Ajmal expands across the GCC, India, and Pakistan. The iconic Dahn Al Oudh range launches and quickly becomes the most gifted fragrance collection in the Gulf, synonymous with celebration.
12 Countries entered in a single decade
2010s
300 fragrances and growing
Ajmal's in-house perfumers — trained in Grasse, France and carrying ancestral knowledge — create a portfolio spanning from ancient attars to modern eau de parfums, winning international awards.
300+ Fragrances crafted in-house
2024 →
The AI & AR chapter
Ajmal enters the next era — where every customer gets a personalised Scent DNA, where bottles come alive in augmented reality, and where 70 years of heritage meets the intelligence of tomorrow.
50+ Countries, 1 family's vision
