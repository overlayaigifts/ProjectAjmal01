1951
The first shop in Ajman
Haji Ajmal Ali opens a modest attar shop in the UAE — hand-blending traditional Arabian fragrances for the local community.
1 Shop, one family, one obsession
1970s
The oud expeditions begin
Ajmal's founders travel personally to Assam, India — forging direct relationships with oud farmers and establishing ethical sourcing practices.
40+ Years of direct farmer partnerships
1990s
From regional to international
Ajmal expands across the GCC, India, and Pakistan. The iconic Dahn Al Oudh range launches and quickly becomes the most gifted fragrance in the Gulf.
12 Countries entered in a single decade
2010s
300 fragrances and growing
In-house perfumers trained in Grasse and carrying ancestral knowledge create a portfolio spanning ancient attars to modern eau de parfums.
300+ Fragrances crafted in-house
